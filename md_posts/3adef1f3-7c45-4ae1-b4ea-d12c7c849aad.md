## Anecdotes


Diogenes was approached by Alexander the Great, who offered to grant him any wish. Diogenes replied:

> “Stand out of my sunlight.”

---

I once saw a teacher say to a student

> “If I saw you leaning in your chair, I wouldn’t wish to work for you.”

He answered:

> “Don’t worry. I wouldn’t hire you in the first place ”

---

I once asked an anonymous student:

> “What do you do so differently? Why are you so out of distribution?”

After thinking about it, He said :

> “I think it’s because I care more. I always look deeper”
